Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Freshly Ground Pepper
  • Sea Salt

Steps

  1. Slice your tomatoes and mozzarella and layer them onto a platter with the fresh basil leaves.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil
  3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  4. Dizzle balsamic vinegar on top of it
